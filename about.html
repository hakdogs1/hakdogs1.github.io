About Wilson Stride

Where quality meets comfort — since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Wilson Stride began as a small local business with one big goal — to create shoes that blend premium quality, comfort, and timeless design. We started with just a few styles, but our passion for creativity and customer satisfaction helped us grow into a trusted footwear brand.

Each pair we make goes through a careful selection of materials, crafting, and quality checks to ensure that every shoe you wear feels as great as it looks.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, stylish, and comfortable shoes that bring confidence to every individual. We aim to make footwear simple yet impactful for everyone.

Our Vision

To become a leading footwear brand known for durability, affordability, and authenticity, inspiring people through what they wear.
